Gary Goodspeed

Origin: Earth

Gary is a goofy yet determined astronaut and the protagonist of Final Space. He accidentally destroyed 92 star cruisers, leading to his imprisonment aboard a spaceship. Despite his clumsy nature, Gary's courage and loyalty drive him to save the universe. His deep connection with Mooncake, his found family, and his love for Quinn fuel his adventures.
